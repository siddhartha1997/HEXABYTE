The tram represents the very spirit of our City of Joy...
a slow yet peaceful tram ride in the evening,
down the streets of North Calcutta
with the smell of new and old books from College Street
or the aroma of Coffee House,
the very concrete presence of Calcutta & Presidency University,
beholding the sights of bargainning at Hatibagan market
reflects the Cultural Heritage as of our Kolkata..
or, perhaps s tram ride from Esplanade to Maidan in the winter,
with someone special beholding the beautiful Victoria Memorial,
and the lush green Maidan,
engaged in all sorts of sports may just be the perfect Romantic date....
Just like Darjeeling won't be Darjeeling without its toy train,
Kolkata won't be Kolkata without its tram rides.......
Come lets explore the world of Kolkata Tram.........
